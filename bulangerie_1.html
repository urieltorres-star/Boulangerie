Bienvenue à Le Boulangeie

Experience the charm of a classic French bistro in the heart of the city. At Chez Lumière, we celebrate simple, elegant cuisine prepared with fresh, seasonal ingredients and served in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Join us for an intimate dinner, a leisurely lunch, or a glass of wine and dessert à la française.
